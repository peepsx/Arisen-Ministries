Instead, our children are suffocated with lies about the beauty of socialism and the brilliance of leftist ideas, which are used to pull them away from God. We're actively working to put God back in the classroom while pushing to take the socialist curriculum out of it.

We're utilizing venues being created at Peeps to spread the Word of God in ways never before thought possible.

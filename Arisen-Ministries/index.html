Fighting For Freedom, Faith & Family.

Arisen Ministries is a modern-day Christian movement leading a digital crusade that is fighting to preserve our God-given inalienable rights on the Internet, along with a mounting battle for justice, life, faith and family across America.

Preserving The Family.

The family unit in America is under attack by a court system that is dehumanizing the American family, and separating children from those they love. We're taking a stand for your God-given right to be a parent and taking our fight to State law makers around the country in our fight for family unity. More About Family Unity

Fighting Injustice

Our fight for prisoners around America goes far beyond our ministry - it's personal. Arisen Ministries was originally founded by prisoners and its ministry was literally created from within a federal prison. We're actively fighting for criminal justice and prison reform, so that prisoners from around America can hit the ground running, like never before. More About Justice Reform

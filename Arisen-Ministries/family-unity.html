Restoring the American family.

The American family is what has made America so special, so powerful and so Godly. In recent years, it has come under attack by a corrupt family court system and we're fighting back in order to restore America's family unit.

Learn more

Keeping the family together.

In today's America, it's normal to see children being born our of wedlock, divorce billboards lining our highways and greedy family attorneys enriching themselves off the destruction of American families. Without a doubt, our connected world has increased the likelihood of infidelity, and the ability to reap a partner's life savings by bearing false witness has introduced a kind of greed that is tearing apart families at their very core. It's a practice that must be stopped and one that many organizations, including churches, are scared to speak out about. The removal of God from our society has made the practice almost universally accepted where the only way to keep the American family together, is to push God back into our society as well as our family court system. It's a battle we're actively fighting.

keyboard_arrow_down

Fighting for father's rights.

While a mother's rights are just as important as a father's rights, more often than not, it's a father's rights that come under attack in family court rooms around America. While it is said that family court cases are "in the best interest of the child," they are many times in the financial interest of the other party and often used to destroy of the life of the losing party. This obvious destruction of a child's parental figure is un-American and the antithesis of what we believe as Christians. For this reason, we're coming to the aid of fathers around the country and fighting to ensure that family courts do indeed act in the best interest of children by keeping all loving and caring parental figures in their lives, on an equal basis.

keyboard_arrow_down

Reforming our family laws.

The family law statutes in many of America's states were drafted to fan the flames of division and divide American families from around the country. Laws must be reformed to support the maximum inclusion of loving parents into the lives of their children, the inclusion of Christian values into the legal process and the creation of processes for bringing broken families back together. Without reform, the very soul of America, the American family will continue to be ripped apart and the destruction of our nation will continue.

Preaching the values of unity.

We believe it is the job of the church, not the government, to preach the values of unit, especially family unity during these last days. The fellowship of family is crucial to our survival as humans, as well as our Christian lifestyles and our Christian journey. The church has failed in large part to fight the pandemic of divorce and to combat America's sexual revolution that has ultimately resulted in the birth of millions of children out of wedlock. We must combat these issues or the divorce pandemic and America's sexual revolution will devour what is left of what was once one nation, under God.
